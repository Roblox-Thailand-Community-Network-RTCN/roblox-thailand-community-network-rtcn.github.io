We're excited to announce a joint collaboration between Roblox Thailand Community & Network (RTCN) and [Sriwisa Studio](https://www.sriwisa.com) on creating a Roblox experience called **Songkran Festival: Flowing Forward**, a project similar to Egg Hunts but Songkran-themed!

## What is this event about?

Songkran Festival: Flowing Forward is an event that has featured and collaborated with **10 experiences** on the Roblox platform. The event was created in celebration of the annual Songkran festival in Thailand, showcasing the tradition in a fun and engaging way for everyone.

This event allows everyone to enjoy Songkran in a digital world and opens the door for possibilities of players from around the world to experience this festival through Roblox with our event in a new way.

### When does the event begin?

The event is scheduled to begin, with the hub experience opening up on the **10th of April, 2026, until the 25th of April, 2026.**

### How do I participate?

You can participate by joining our [Roblox experience](https://www.roblox.com/games/119191526138013) and [creating a Roblox account](https://www.roblox.com/CreateAccount) if you haven't already.

Here are some details of what you need to look out for:

- **Collect Badges:** Complete quests in various Roblox experiences to earn badges. These badges can be used to claim your prize once all of them have been collected!
- **Explore:** Explore our hub experience and our collaborated experiences for clues, special items, a new fun game to add to your library, and hang out with friends!
- **Earn prizes:** You can earn up to **2 free UGC accessories** when completing the quests in this event.
